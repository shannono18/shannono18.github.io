A little bit about me.

I've spent most of my career helping employers understand and get the most out of SaaS platforms, in support of their overall well-being and healthcare strategies. Upon completion of UC Irvine's full-stack development bootcamp in October 2021, I'll have earned my certificate of completion in web development. I'm excited to make a full switch to front-end development and build upon my work experience as a Customer Success Manager, project manager, cross-functional collaborator and problem solver.
